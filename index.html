About This Event

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, MES College of Engineering Kuttippuram is organizing the second edition of International Conference on "Futuristic Technologies in Control Systems & Renewable Energy" (ICFCR 2024) on 25 and 26 of September 2024 in Hybrid mode. The conference is technically co-sponsored by IEEE Kerala Section. The conference aims to bring together leading researchers, practi ers, practitioners and educators to discuss the most recent innovations, trends, concerns, practical challenges and to seek solutions which can be adopted in the field of control and renewable energy. This conference will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge in theory, methodology and application of control systems in power engineering and renewable energy systems. The participants will acquire knowledge about current technological developments in the field of modeling, simulation and control of power and renewable energy systems.
